MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT - ACME CORP & TECH SOLUTIONS LTD

1. Pricing and Rates

The agreed daily rate for Senior Engineering services is $1,200 USD.
Junior Engineering services are billed at $800 USD.
Any hours worked on weekends (Saturday/Sunday) shall be billed at 1.5x the standard rate.

2. Payment Terms

Standard payment terms are Net 45 days from receipt of valid invoice.
Early Payment Discount: If Client pays within 10 days, a 2% discount applies to the total invoice amount.

3. Volume Rebates

If total spend exceeds $50,000 USD in a calendar quarter, a retrospective volume rebate of 5% applies to all spend in that quarter.

4. Expenses

Travel expenses are capped at $500 per month unless pre-approved in writing.
